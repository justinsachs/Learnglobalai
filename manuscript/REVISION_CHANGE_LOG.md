# ADAPT OR DIE: Manuscript Revision Change Log

## Overview

This document provides a detailed record of all revisions made to the ADAPT OR DIE manuscript in response to the editorial review. The revisions are organized by phase and chapter.

## Revision Principles

All revisions were guided by the following principles, derived from the editorial review:

1.  **Preserve all existing content.** No previously written content was removed.
2.  **Add depth, not length.** Every addition was aimed at increasing the substance, credibility, and narrative force of the manuscript.
3.  **Calibrate factual claims.** All statistics and research findings were verified against original sources and appropriately qualified.
4.  **Add steelman counterarguments.** The strongest possible objections to the book's thesis were identified and addressed directly.
5.  **Add concrete case studies.** Named, real-world examples were added to illustrate the principles of Adaptive Education™ in action.
6.  **Establish a canonical definition.** A single, clear, and consistent definition of "Adaptive Education™" was added to the manuscript.

## Phase 1: Structural Integrity and Factual Calibration
