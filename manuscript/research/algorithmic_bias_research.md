## Key Takeaways for Book

1.  **The Personalization Paradox:** The very act of personalizing learning can inadvertently reinforce and even amplify existing inequalities.
2.  **Bias is Baked In:** AI models are trained on data from the real world, and the real world is full of bias. That bias gets baked into the models.
3.  **The Risk of Tracking:** If not designed carefully, adaptive learning can create a new form of tracking, where students from disadvantaged backgrounds are funneled into less challenging, remedial pathways.
4.  **Equity by Design:** The only way to avoid this is to be intentional about designing for equity from the very beginning.
5.  **The Human in the Loop is Essential:** AI can be a powerful tool for equity, but it cannot be left on its own. Human oversight, judgment, and compassion are essential.
