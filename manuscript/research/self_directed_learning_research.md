## Lifelong Learning

### Point Loma Nazarene University

**Habits:** Outlines 10 habits of a lifelong learner, including reading voraciously, embracing change, and staying curious.[^7]

### IACET (April 2020)

**Mindset:** "Lifelong learning is a concept that goes beyond our education system and urges people to **keep an open-minded and be curious** no matter how old you are."[^8]

### Full Focus

**Everything is an Education:** "Lifelong learners see everything as an education. Every book they read, every podcast they listen to, is a chance to grow and improve."[^9]

## Key Takeaways for Book

1.  **You Are the CEO of Your Own Learning:** In the new economy, you can no longer afford to outsource your learning to anyone else. You have to take ownership of your own development.
2.  **The ADAPT Framework is Your Personal Operating System:** The same ADAPT framework that organizations use to build adaptive learning programs can be used by individuals to manage their own learning.
3.  **Build Your Personal SourcePack:** Curate your own library of trusted resources—the books, blogs, podcasts, and people that you turn to for reliable information.
4.  **Create Your Own Proofs:** Don't just learn things. Build things. Create a portfolio of proof that shows what you can do.
5.  **Your Network is Your University:** Build a Personal Learning Network (PLN) of people who can support you, challenge you, and help you to grow.
