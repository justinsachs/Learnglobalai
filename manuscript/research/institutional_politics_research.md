## Why Innovation Fails in Education

### EdWeek (February 2025)

**Distraction from What Matters:** "The fascination with innovation is a huge problem. I think it **distracts from what matters**—whether a policy or practice makes sense—and instead..."[^7]

### Hoover Institution (February 2026)

**Failure to Scale:** New research reveals why education innovation fails to scale and what can be done about it, building on the “Ours versus Theirs” framework.[^8]

### Primary Goal

**Lack of Capacity:** "EdTech keeps failing because **schools lack capacity to implement it**."[^9]

## Key Takeaways for Book

1.  **Innovation is Not Enough:** A good idea is not enough. A better technology is not enough. To succeed, you have to navigate the complex and often irrational world of institutional politics.
2.  **The Immune System of the Organization:** Established organizations have a powerful immune system that is designed to reject anything new or different. Your innovation will be seen as a threat.
3.  **Identify Your Stakeholders:** You need to understand who has the power to say yes, who has the power to say no, and what they care about.
4.  **Speak Their Language:** You have to be able to translate your vision into a language that your stakeholders can understand and support. You have to show them how your innovation will help them achieve their goals.
5.  **Build a Coalition:** You cannot do it alone. You need to build a coalition of supporters who will champion your cause and help you to overcome the inevitable resistance.
