# Time-to-Competency and ROI of Adaptive Learning Research

## Time-to-Competency: The New ROI

### SHIFT eLearning

**Definition:** "It's the time that passes from the start of training until a **real, measurable mastery of the skill is demonstrated on the job**."[^1]

### eLearning Industry (September 2023)

**Distinction:** "Time to competence" is the time it takes for an individual to become **proficient or skilled**, as opposed to just completing a course.[^2]

### Bigtincan (December 2025)

**Application:** "Time to competency in sales onboarding is a metric that tracks how long it takes a new hire to develop the **skills, knowledge, and process**..."[^3]

## The Cost of Slow Onboarding

### Enboarder

**Key Insight:** The cost of onboarding a new employee can vary widely, but a significant portion of that cost is the **lost productivity** during the ramp-up period.[^4]

### Appical (October 2025)

**Productivity Loss:** "A key challenge in onboarding is managing the **Time-to-Full-Productivity (TfFP)**." Slow starts are a major hidden cost.[^5]

### Docebo (January 2026)

**The Invisible Cost:** "A solid onboarding plan **reduces ramp-time**, improves retention, increases profitability, and helps new hires adopt your culture—fast."[^6]

### Patty Crawford Consulting (March 2025)

**Cost of Replacement:** "Replacing an employee can cost anywhere from **50% to 200% of their annual salary**, factoring in recruitment, training, and lost productivity."[^7]

## The ROI of Adaptive Learning

### eLearning Industry (May 2021)

**Key Benefit:** "By applying adaptive techniques, you benefit from **analytics offering insights into training**, unlike nonadaptive programs."[^8]

### Mindsmith.ai (April 2025)

**Case Study:** An adaptive learning initiative **saved 391 training hours** and forecasted a **600% return on investment (ROI)**.[^9]

## Key Takeaways for Book

1.  **Time-to-Competency is the Key Metric:** In corporate training, the only metric that matters is how quickly you can get an employee to a state of productive competence.
2.  **Slow Onboarding is Expensive:** The cost of a new hire is not just their salary. It is also the cost of their lost productivity while they are learning the ropes.
3.  **Adaptive Learning Accelerates Time-to-Competency:** By personalizing the learning path, adaptive learning can significantly reduce the time it takes for an employee to become proficient.
4.  **The ROI is Massive:** The ROI of adaptive learning is not just in reduced training hours, but in increased productivity, higher retention, and a more agile workforce.
