# Credential Engine 2025 Fact-Check

**Source:** Credential Engine, "Counting Credentials 2025 Report"

**URL:** https://credentialengine.org/all-resources/2025-counting-credentials/

**Key Findings:**

*   **Total Unique Credentials:** 1,850,034 in the U.S.
*   **Credential Categories:**
    *   Degrees: 264,099
    *   Certificates: 486,352
    *   Certifications: 6,892
    *   Micro-credentials: 3,384
    *   Licenses: 14,331
    *   Secondary School Diplomas: 52,948
    *   Badges: 1,022,028
*   **Providers:** 134,491
*   **Annual Expenditures:** $2.34 trillion

**Implication for Manuscript:** The claim of "1.5 million unique non-degree credentials" is outdated and inaccurate. The correct number is 1.85 million, and it includes degrees. The revision must reflect this new data and the specific categories.
