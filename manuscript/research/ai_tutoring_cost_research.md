# AI Tutoring Cost Research

**Source:** Brookings Institution, "What the research shows about generative AI in tutoring" (Jan 27, 2026)

**URL:** https://www.brookings.edu/articles/what-the-research-shows-about-generative-ai-in-tutoring/

**Key Findings:**

*   **Cost per pupil:** Approximately $48
*   **Marginal cost:** Estimated at $9 ($124 per pupil over 4 academic quarters)
*   **Comparison to human tutoring:** AI tutoring is significantly cheaper. While human tutors charge $30-100+ per hour, AI tutoring is a fraction of that cost.
*   **Value Proposition:** AI tutoring offers a path to solve the challenge of individualized education at scale, providing personalized learning opportunities that were previously only available to the privileged few.
*   **Hybrid Model:** The most effective model combines AI tutoring with human teacher support, leveraging the strengths of both.
