# Course Completion and Effectiveness Statistics

## Online Course Completion Rates

### MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses)
- **5-15%** completion rate for free online courses
- **15-40%** completion rate for paid courses
- **90%** dropout rate commonly cited for MOOCs
- Students with online learning communities are **5x more engaged**
- Students with community support are **16x more likely to complete** programs

**Source:** Newzenler Blog, "How Online Communities Are Revolutionising Course Completion Rates" (2025)

### Success Case: Harvard Business School Online
- Introduced social learning elements
- Completion rates increased to **over 85%**
- Key factor: connection, not just content quality

**Source:** Newzenler Blog (2025)

## Corporate Training Effectiveness

### Skills Gap
- **70%** of employees report they don't have mastery of skills needed for their jobs
- Only **12%** of employees apply new skills learned in L&D programs to their work

**Source:** Shift E-Learning, "10 Statistics on Corporate Training"

### Training ROI
- Companies with formalized training yield **218% higher income per employee**
- **17%** increase in productivity when employees receive targeted training
- **21%** boost in profitability with targeted training

**Source:** Devlin Peck, "Employee Training Statistics" (2025); Harvard Business School Online (2025)

### Employee Retention
- **76%** of employees more inclined to stay if company offers continuous learning
- **80%+** of HR professionals recognize importance of training

**Source:** MyShortlister, "90+ Employee Training Statistics in 2025"

## Online Learning Industry Growth

- Expected CAGR of **8.20%** from 2025 to 2029
- **9.1%** annual growth projected between 2018-2026
- **30%** of U.S. students enrolled in at least one online course (2025)
- **63%** of high school students have taken online courses

**Sources:** Wooclap (2025); Devlin Peck (2025); AIU (2025)

## Key Insights for Book

1. **Completion Crisis:** Traditional online education has a massive completion problem (85-95% dropout for free courses)
2. **Skills Transfer Gap:** Even when employees complete training, only 12% apply it to work
3. **The Competence Gap:** 70% of employees lack mastery of required skills despite training availability
4. **Community Effect:** Social learning increases completion 16x, suggesting personalization alone isn't enough
5. **ROI Exists:** When training works, it delivers measurable business outcomes (218% income increase, 21% profitability boost)
