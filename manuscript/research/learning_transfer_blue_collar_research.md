# Learning Transfer in Blue-Collar and Field Work Research

## Learning Transfer

### Training Orchestra (November 2025)

**Definition:** "Transfer of training refers to the systematic process of developing practical skills training for specific job responsibilities."[^1]

### The eLearning Coach

**Core Concept:** "Learning transfer refers to the act of **applying knowledge and skills learned in one situation to a new situation or context**."[^2]

### Training Magazine (February 2014)

**Application:** "Learning transfer can be defined as the ability of a learner to successfully **apply the behavior, knowledge, and skills acquired in a learning event to the job**."[^3]

## Adaptive Learning for Skilled Trades

### ADTC

**Personalization:** "Adaptive Learning **tailors training to each candidate's level and ability**."[^4]

### MapleLMS

**Effectiveness:** "Yes, it is highly effective in technical and vocational education because it **tailors training to different skill levels and provides real-time feedback**."[^5]

### ABC Carolinas (February 2026)

**Gamified Training:** "Adaptive learning pathways will create **responsive curricula** in which success in certain modules automatically unlocks more advanced content."[^6]

## AR/VR in Blue-Collar Training

### Kompanions (June 2025)

**Immersive Learning:** "AR/VR offers **hands-on, immersive training that improves retention, reduces risk, and accelerates learning**—ideal for practical roles in manufacturing, construction, and logistics."[^7]

### Academii

**Safe Practice:** "VR simulations allow workers to **practice and refine their skills in a safe, controlled environment**."[^8]

### Medium (Ganesh Mohan)

**Real-Time Guidance:** "AR is transforming the way workers learn and perform their jobs, providing **real-time, hands-on guidance that boosts efficiency, reduces errors, and enhances safety**."[^9]

## Key Takeaways for Book

1.  **In Field Work, Transfer is Everything:** For blue-collar and field workers, the only thing that matters is whether they can apply what they learned in the classroom to the job site.
2.  **The Classroom is Not the Job Site:** The context of the classroom is fundamentally different from the context of the job site. This makes learning transfer a major challenge.
3.  **Adaptive Learning Bridges the Gap:** By personalizing the learning path and providing real-time feedback, adaptive learning can help to bridge the gap between the classroom and the job site.
4.  **AR/VR are Game-Changers for Transfer:** Immersive technologies like AR and VR allow workers to practice their skills in a realistic, safe environment, which dramatically improves learning transfer.
5.  **The Future of Blue-Collar Training is Adaptive and Immersive:** The combination of adaptive learning and immersive technologies is the key to unlocking the potential of the blue-collar workforce.
