# Adaptive Learning in High-Stakes Domains Research

## Safety Training

### RelyOn

**AI-Powered Adaptive Learning:** "Through the use of AI-powered adaptive learning software, the courses will automatically **adapt the content, pace, and assessments to optimize knowledge**..."[^1]

### ScienceDirect (2023) - Xu et al.

**Bayesian-based Knowledge Tracing:** An adaptive learning framework for construction safety training that uses a Bayesian-based knowledge tracing model to adjust the training content based on the trainee's knowledge state.[^2]

### Discover Artificial Intelligence (2024) - Karimi et al.

**Maritime Safety Training:** A systematic literature review of AI-based adaptive instructional systems for maritime safety training, highlighting the potential for personalized and more effective training.[^3]

## Healthcare Education

### PMC (2017) - Sharma et al.

**Mastery of Learning:** "Adaptive learning could generate **personalized learning content to improve mastery of learning** and connect learners and faculty to one another..."[^4]

### AMA

**Lifelong Learning:** The AMA's adaptive learning resources prepare today's medical students for **lifelong learning in the changing health care system**.[^5]

### TiberHealth

## Compliance Training

### NAVEX

**Personalized by Role and Behavior:** "NAVEX's Adaptive Learning courses **adapt the content to the individual learner based on their performance** throughout the training."[^7]

### LRN (December 2023)

**Understanding the Nuances:** "Adaptive learning focuses on **understanding the nuances of your learners and adapting the content to them** during training."[^8]

### Learning Pool (August 2025)

**Boosting Engagement and Reducing Risk:** "Adaptive compliance training **personalizes learning by role and behavior**—boosting engagement, reducing risk, and driving stronger ROI."[^9]

## Key Takeaways for Book

1.  **In High-Stakes Domains, Competence is Not Optional:** In fields like safety, healthcare, and compliance, the cost of incompetence can be catastrophic.
2.  **One-Size-Fits-All is a Recipe for Disaster:** A one-size-fits-all approach to training in these domains is not just inefficient; it is dangerous.
3.  **Adaptive Learning Ensures Mastery:** Adaptive learning is the only way to ensure that every learner achieves true mastery of the critical competencies.
4.  **Personalization Reduces Risk:** By personalizing the learning path, you can identify and remediate knowledge gaps that could otherwise lead to costly or tragic errors.
5.  **The Audit Trail is Crucial:** In these highly regulated domains, the ability to produce an auditable record of competence (the Competence Ledger) is essential.
