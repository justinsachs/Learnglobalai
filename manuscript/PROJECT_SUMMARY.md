### Key Frameworks and Concepts

1. **The ADAPT Framework**
   - **A**udit: Learner Reality Is Data
   - **D**efine: Outcomes Must Be Proofs
   - **A**ssemble: Source Truth Controls Hallucinations
   - **P**ersonalize: Paths and Remediation Loops
   - **T**ransfer: The Only Metric That Matters

2. **The Competence Ledger**
   - System of record for verified competencies
   - Immutable, auditable, and evidence-based
   - Replaces traditional LMS completion tracking

3. **The SourcePack**
   - Canonical, version-controlled source of truth
   - Grounds AI generation in vetted content
   - Prevents hallucinations and ensures accuracy

4. **The Coaching Loop**
   - Detect misconceptions
   - Provide targeted remediation
   - Verify mastery before progression

5. **Proof Hours vs. Credit Hours**
   - Shift from time-based to competence-based credentials
   - Evidence portfolios over completion certificates
   - Market-valued proof of skills
