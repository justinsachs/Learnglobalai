## CHAPTER 2 — Tutor Abundance: The Economics of Attention Has Flipped

**Purpose:** Make “tutor abundance” the irreversible driver of Adaptive Education™.

**Opening hook:** A learner asks a question at 11:47 PM and receives an explanation, practice problems, and correction loops—instantly.

**Core claims:**
- Every learner can now have a 24/7 coach that explains, quizzes, corrects misconceptions, and adapts pacing without shame or delay.
- The biggest shift isn’t “answers”—it’s correction and practice design.
- Humans still do best: motivation, identity, meaning, social belonging.

**Key sections:**
- Tutor abundance is real
- What abundant coaching unlocks: misconception correction + shame-free practice loops
- The danger of “answer machines” and dependency

**Exhibits:**
- Exhibit: The Coaching Loop (ask → attempt → feedback → remediation → retry)

**Operator Playbook:** How to design coaching prompts and misconception checks (capability-based, not tool-specific).

**Failure Modes:**
- Tutor that “answers” instead of teaches (dependency)
- Tutor that isn’t grounded (trust collapse)

**Proof Task:** Convert 1 lesson into a coach-driven practice loop (3 misconceptions + 3 micro-drills).

**Pull-quote:** “When tutoring becomes abundant, the bottleneck moves to verification.”
